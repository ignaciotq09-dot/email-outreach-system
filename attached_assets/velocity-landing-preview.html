How does the AI personalization work?

Our AI analyzes publicly available information about your prospects, including company news, social media activity, and professional backgrounds to craft unique, relevant messages for each recipient.

Yes! VELOCITY integrates seamlessly with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and 20+ other popular CRMs. You can sync contacts, track activities, and update records automatically.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never share or sell your data, and you maintain full ownership of all your contacts and content.

What's your deliverability rate?

Our customers typically see 95%+ deliverability rates. We use advanced warming techniques, domain authentication, and reputation monitoring to ensure your emails reach the inbox.
